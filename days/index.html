    Treasure Island A branching choose-your-path treasure hunt. day 3

    Highest Score Find the top score in a list. day 5

    Password Generator Random password from letters, numbers, and symbols. day 5

    Blackjack The first capstone, a full game of blackjack. day 11 - capstone
